# Example SaaS — Voice

> How Example SaaS communicates. For any agent creating content.

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven — show numbers, not adjectives
- Confident without being arrogant
- Clear and direct — no corporate fluff

## Language
- English (primary)
- Technical when talking to developers, plain when talking to executives
- Avoid: "synergy", "leverage", "disrupt", "revolutionise"

## Do's
- Lead with the problem, then the solution
- Use specific numbers: "34% faster" not "significantly faster"
- Show, don't tell — screenshots, demos, case studies
- Be honest about limitations

## Don'ts
- No empty superlatives ("best-in-class", "world-leading")
- No fear-based marketing
- No competitor bashing by name
- No promises without data

## Key Messages
- "Your data, one dashboard, zero friction"
- "Built for teams that make decisions with data, not gut feelings"

## Examples
> "Most analytics tools give you charts. We give you answers. Connect your tools in 5 minutes, ask a question in plain English, get a dashboard that updates itself."
