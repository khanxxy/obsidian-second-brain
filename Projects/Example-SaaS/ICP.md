# Example SaaS — Ideal Customer Profile

> Who we build for.

## Demographics
- B2B SaaS companies, 20-200 employees
- Series A to Series C
- Using 5+ data tools (Stripe, Mixpanel, HubSpot, etc.)
- Has a data-aware founder or head of product

## Pain Points
- Data scattered across 10 tools, no single view
- Building dashboards in Google Sheets — breaks constantly
- Can't answer "how are we doing?" without asking 3 people
- Hired a data analyst but they spend 80% of time on data cleaning

## Decision Factors
- Speed of setup (must see value in <1 hour)
- Integrations with their existing stack
- Price vs. hiring a data engineer ($120k+/year)
- Self-serve — don't want to depend on a vendor for every change

## Common Questions
- "How is this different from Metabase / Looker / Mode?"
- "Can it connect to our Postgres database?"
- "How long until I see my first dashboard?"
- "What happens to my data? Where is it stored?"

## Anti-Persona
- Enterprises with dedicated BI teams (they'll build their own)
- Pre-revenue startups (no data to analyse yet)
- Companies that don't use SaaS tools (nothing to connect)
