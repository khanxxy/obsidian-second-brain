# Backlog

Planned work that is not active yet.

Active work belongs in [[NOW]]. Project-specific next steps belong in the project's `HANDOFF.md`.

---

## Conventions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Ready | Can start now |
| Open | Idea is clear, plan is missing |
| Raw | Rough note, may be dropped |

| Effort | Meaning |
|---|---|
| S | Less than 2 hours |
| M | 1-2 days |
| L | Weeks |
| XL | Months |
